PrimeCare Concierge — Telemedicine Product Management Case Study

**Author:** Ekezie Francess
**Date:** June 23, 2026  
**Role:** Technical Product Manager / Product Strategist

## Executive Summary
**PrimeCare** is a premium, asset-light medical concierge service layer in Nigeria designed to marry world-class clinical safety with elite hospitality and absolute privacy.
The platform targets high-net-worth individuals (HNWIs) and the African diaspora seeking specialized elective procedures (e.g., aesthetics, body contouring/BBL) and premium health management.

##  The Problem
* **Trust & Safety Deficit:** High market fragmentation with unregulated clinics and inconsistent care standards, causing intense patient anxiety
* **No Luxury Aftercare:** A total absence of high-end, secure, and private clinical recovery environments in Nigeria
* **Logistical Friction:** Enormous operational hurdles for overseas patients, causing massive outbound capital flight to foreign healthcare hubs

## The Solution
* **Vetted Partner Network:** An uncompromised, invite-only directory of the top board-certified plastic surgeons in Lagos
* **Asset-Light Managed Hospitality:** Partnering on-demand with premium, secure serviced apartments in Ikoyi/VI equipped with 24/7 specialized private nursing care
* **"Luxury Bubble" Logistics:** End-to-end client synchronization, including airport VIP fast-track protocols, private chauffeurs, and bespoke post-op nutrition[

## Key Frameworks Inside

### MoSCoW Feature Prioritization
* **Must Have:** Curated Surgeon Directory, HIPAA-Compliant Intake, On-Demand Recovery Suites, 24/7 Private Nursing, Discreet Logistics
* **Won't Have (Phase 1):** Proprietary Mobile Apps, Permanent Real Estate Purchases, Automated AI Doctor Matchmaking

###  12-Month Product Roadmap
* **Months 1–3 (MVP Launch):** Finalize clinical SLAs, launch low-code secure intake portals, and execute a 5-client closed beta
* **Months 4–6 (Optimization):** Standardize specialized suite equipment, onboard private culinary networks, and scale diaspora marketing
* **Months 7–12 (Scale & Tech):** Launch a custom proprietary Client Web Portal, transition to long-term boutique leases, and expand regionally into Ghana and Côte d'Ivoire

## Core Success Metrics (KPIs)
* **NPS:** 95+ target with a strict 0% Privacy or Anonymity Breach Rate.
* **Efficiency:** Under 2-hour digital inquiry response time and 100% on-time logistical synchronization.
* **Financials:** Gross Profit Margins of 35–45% per package and an LTV:CAC ratio > 4:1
